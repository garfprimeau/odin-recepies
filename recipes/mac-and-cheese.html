Slow Cooker Mac and Cheese

Description

This crockpot mac and cheese recipe is creamy, comforting, and takes just moments to assemble in a slow cooker.

Great for large family gatherings and to take to potluck dinners. It's always a big hit!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package of elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1 (5 ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs, well beaten
  • 2 cups, whole milk
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cheddar cheese soup (such as Campbell's)
  • 1 pinch paprika, or as desired (optional)

    • Steps

    1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in macaroni and return to a boil.

      Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.

      Drain and transfer pasta to a slow cooker.

    2. Add butter to pasta and stir until melted;season with salt and pepper.

      Sprinkle about 1/2 of the cheddar cheese over pasta and stir.

    3. Whisk evaporated milk and eggs together in a bowl until smooth;stir into pasta mixture.
    4. Sprinkle remaining cheese over pasta mixture;garnish with paprika.
    5. Cook on low for 3 hours.
    6. Serve hot and emjoy!
